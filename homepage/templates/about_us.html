Our Story

Walking The Talk

Welcome to Walkoria, your number one source for premium women's branded shoes. We are dedicated to providing you the very best of women's footwear, with an emphasis on unmatched luxury, durability, and trend-setting feminine designs that stand out.

Founded with a bold vision to revolutionize the online footwear shopping experience specifically for women, Walkoria has grown from a dream into a premier destination for branded footwear. Our relentless passion for empowering women through eco-friendly, stylish, and high-performance shoes drove us to establish a curated collection that perfectly blends modern chic with irresistible comfort. Today, we proudly serve empowered women all around the world!

Sneha Venugopal

Founder & CEO

"I started Walkoria because I believe that the right pair of shoes can empower you to conquer anything. Fashion should not compromise comfort, and luxury should not be unattainable. Every pair in our collection is curated with love, keeping your journey in mind."
